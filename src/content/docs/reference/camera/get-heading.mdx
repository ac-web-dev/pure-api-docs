---
title: getHeading
---

Get the heading of the camera in degrees.

```lua
pure.camera.getHeading()
```

Returns the camera heading in degrees, in the range `0`-`360`.
`0°` corresponds to North and increases clockwise.

:::note

Angles use compass rotation (clockwise from North).
To obtain a direction vector using `sin`/`cos`, convert the angle first.
See the [guide on working with camera heading vectors](/guides/working-with-camera-heading-vectors).

:::
